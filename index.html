    Vagrant with Ubuntu Focal (20.04)

    View Google Earth in the browser

    Google now supports Google Earth in Google Maps. As far as I know it works in Chrome and Chromium. If Google Earth isn’t available where the satellite view options is, your graphic card is in the browsers blacklist. To force enabling WebGL in the browser start it with the following options chromium-browser --enable-webgl --ignore-gpu-blacklist Now you can use Google Earth in maps.google.com.
